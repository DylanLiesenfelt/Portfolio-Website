About Me

Hello there! I'm a 27-year-old with a diverse journey that has shaped who I am today. I proudly served in the Navy as a Petty Officer 2nd Class Aviation Electrican's Mate for 8 years, an experience that instilled in me discipline, teamwork, and a strong sense of duty. After my time in the service, I set my sights on the world of technology and became a passionate software engineer.

My days are spent crafting lines of code and turning complex challenges into elegant solutions. I thrive on the ever-evolving nature of the tech world, constantly seeking to expand my skills and embrace new technologies. From problem-solving to creating innovative applications, I find immense satisfaction in bringing ideas to life through the power of code.

Beyond the screen and keyboard, you'll often find me outdoors, exploring the world alongside my loyal husky companion, Forrest. Whether it's hiking through breathtaking landscapes or simply enjoying a leisurely stroll, our adventures together are a cherished part of my life. Forrest's boundless energy and unwavering loyalty remind me of the importance of staying active, staying true to oneself, and embracing the journey.

As someone who has experienced the camaraderie of military service, the intel, and the joy of a four-legged friend's companionship, I'm excited to see what new opportunities life has in store. Join me as I continue to learn, grow, and create.
